About Us

The Village Nut Company Limited deals with processing of macadamia nuts using the finest selected nuts. The branded products constitutes non-genetically modified, naturally grown macadamia nuts from the central highlands of Kenya. The nut was introduced to Kenyan farmers in 1946 and its commercial production started in 1960’s. Since 1982, the acreage under macadamia has increased drastically in the sprawling slopes of Mt Kenya.

The construction of the factory begun in late 2013 and was completed by early 2014 when we started macadamia nut processing.

Nursery

From seed to seedling. We graft our Macadamia seedlings, which we then sell to farmers who then become our source of the Macadamia we process.

Production

After acquiring the Macadamia, we blow, wash & dry, crack, sort, grade and finally pack for our clients.

Quality Control

We check for mould, old stock, insect damage among other things to ensure the best quality Macadamia for our clients.
