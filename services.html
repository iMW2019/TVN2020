HOW WE PROCESS

QC CHECK

Hand selected for the best grade.

BLOWING

Cold air is introduced to gradually bring down moisture content

WASHING & DRYING

Washed and then put into the drier. Heat is gradually introduced to bring down moisture from 30% to 1.5%.

CRACKING

Cracked mechanically to different sizes.

SORTING & GRADING

Seperating shell from kernel, done by hand and then inspected for quality.

WEIGHING & PACKING

Packed in different sizes, then sealed in air tight Aluminium packs.

WAREHOUSING

The final product is stored in a cool warehouse.

DISPATCH

Shipments are done as per the clients orders.
